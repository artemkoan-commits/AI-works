Welcome to Demon Slayer

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba is set in a dark fantasy version of Taishō-era Japan, where humans fight against deadly demons in a secret war.

About the Anime

The story follows Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy who becomes a demon slayer after his family is slaughtered and his sister is turned into a demon.

Main Characters

Tanjiro

Kind and brave demon slayer.

Nezuko

Tanjiro's sister turned demon.

Zenitsu

Fearful but powerful swordsman.

Inosuke

Wild fighter with boar mask.
